  task       raw(100-1shot)  reachable(o@8-1shot)  lever realized   ceiling ok?

  Reachable headroom UPPER-BOUNDS the lever gain (realized<=reachable for all 4).

  Below the ceiling, realization is lever-dependent: SQL +11/20 (retry~=fresh), MBPP +1/12 (rich
  'fix broken code' feedback ANCHORS), HumanEval +2/2 (tiny ceiling, fully captured).
